EDUC 349: Teaching of Latin, K-12 (2013-2014, Semester 2)

This course introduces students to teaching Latin in grades K-12. Theories, methods, issues, and trends are examined. Other topics include curriculum development and textbook, computer and audio visual resources. Offered upon demand. Arranged with Classics and Education chairs and the Latin instructor. Required field experience component. Prerequisite: Education 330. Offered periodically in the spring semester.
