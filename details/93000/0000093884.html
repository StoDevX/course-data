PHYS 232A: Analytical Physics II (2014-2015, Semester 1)

Physics 232, the third course in the three-semester calculus-based sequence, explores special relativity, waves and oscillations, and the quantum mechanics of light and matter. There is one 2.5 hour laboratory meeting per week. Prerequisites: PHYS 131 or permission of instructor, and concurrent registration in (or previous completion of) MATH 220. Offered annually in the fall semester.
