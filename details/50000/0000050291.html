HIST 194: Global Histories from Ancient Times to 1500 (2006-2007, Semester 1)

Students examine, comparatively and chronologically, the evolution of selected cultures and societies before 1500 in Asia, India, Africa, Europe, the Middle East, and pre-Columbian America. Through original texts, historical studies, and literature, students explore such topics as religious practices and the birth of world religions, trade and interregional exchanges, human interaction with the environment, forms of political authority, and family life and gender roles. Offered most years.
