SPAN 250A: Gateway to the Spanish-Speaking World (2006-2007, Semester 1)

Students explore the topic of family and society in the Spanish-speaking world. They develop critical reading skills by analyzing literary and non-literary texts, including at least one substantive literary work. Students develop skills in descriptive, narrative, expository, and argumentative forms of writing. Taught in Spanish. Prerequisite: Spanish 232 or placement into Spanish 250.
