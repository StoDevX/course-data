ART 221: Oil/Acrylic Painting (2006-2007, Semester 1)

This course develops and stimulates research into the emotional/expressive properties of art-making in the painting media. By investigating thematic compositional and technical problems, students develop a personalized approach to ideas and content. Students learn the importance of process, need for flexibility, how to seek alternatives and how to recognize that a painting has a life of its own. Prerequisites: Art 111 and 112 (or 120), and 113 . Materials fee. Offered annually.
