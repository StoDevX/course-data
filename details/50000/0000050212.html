SPAN 275: Exploring Hispanic Literature (2006-2007, Semester 1)

Introduces students to literary terminology and to principles of literary analysis across genres, including poetry, short stories, theater, and novel, by examining literary works in their socio-historical context. In different semesters, the focus may be literature of the Mexican Revolution, urban and rural life, or other topics chosen by the instructor. Taught in Spanish. Prerequisite: Spanish 250.
