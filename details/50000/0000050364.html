MUSIC 275: Instrumental Chamber Music (2006-2007, Semester 1)

Students study and perform chamber music repertoire for strings, winds, percussion and keyboard. Ensembles formed frequently include string quartets, woodwind quintets or brass quintets. Existing ensembles may enroll, or new groups may be formed. This course requires regular and frequent rehearsals, coaching sessions with faculty and recital performances. May be repeated. Students may earn up to one course credit, but only 0.25 per semester. Prerequisite: permission of instructor. Offered every semester.
