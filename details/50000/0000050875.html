ART 223: Sculpture/Metal Casting (2006-2007, Semester 1)

This sculpture course introduces lost wax bronze casting, an art and industrial process that changed the course of human civilization. Students learn to cast, finish and preset cast metal work, building upon concepts from the foundation course and exploring the next level of sculpture topics, issues and concern. Te course makes use of hands-on instruction, readings, slide talks and discussion. Lab fee. Prerequisite: Art 114 (Mandatory); Art 111, 112, 113, 116(recommended). Offered annually. Fall Semester only.
