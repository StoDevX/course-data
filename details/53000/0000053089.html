HIST 226: Modern France (2006-2007, Semester 1)

This course surveys the history of France since 1774, tracing elements of stability and upheaval from Old Regime to Republic. Emphasis is on the revolutionary tradition and the development of republicanism, social history of the 19th century, de Gaulle and his legacy, and the Socialist experiment. Foreign Language Across the Curriculum course available in French. Offered most years.
