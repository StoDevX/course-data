GERM 231: Intermediate German I (2005-2006, Summer Session 2)

Students explore life in the German-speaking countries through reading and discussion of narrative texts. The course emphasizes vocabulary building, a thorough review of German grammar and the composition of short narratives to develop writing skills. Taught in German with some grammar explanations in English. Prerequisite: German 112 or placement by test. Offered Fall and Spring semesters.
