MUSIC 263: Lyric Diction (2006-2007, Semester 1)

Students study and practice principles for signing in English and Italian with good pronunciation, enunciation and expression. Course content includes phonetic analysis using the International Phonetic Alphabet and identifying and exercising the phonemic features of English and Italian through class performances and listening assignments. Prerequisite: two semesters of voice lessons. Offered Fall Semester, 2004-05 and alternate years.
