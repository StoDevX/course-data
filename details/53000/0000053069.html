GERM 270: German in the Professions (2006-2007, Semester 1)

Examines a wide variety of texts used in a particular professional or disciplinary field such as economics, the sciences, medicine, art, music and theology. Enhances listening and reading skills in specialized areas of the work world. Recommended for students considering an internship in Germany. Taught in German. Prerequisite: one 250-level course (two recommended). May be repeated if topic is different. GE: FOL-G.
