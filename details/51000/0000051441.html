EDUC 260: Perspectives on Teaching (off-campus: student's choice of setting) (2006-2007, Interim)

This course provides practical experiences for students desiring to explore career opportunities Students, primarily sophomores, live off-campus and participate in different observational and practical experiences in schools of their choice. Students are placed in schools for the full day and are expected to participate in evening and weekend activities. First-year students with special circumstances should consult Education Department chair. Applications due Oct. 15. P/N only. Offered during Interim.
