FREN 233: Language and Culture Studies in The French Caribbean (abroad) (2006-2007, Interim)

Students travel to the Caribbean island of Martinique to study French language and French-Caribbean culture at the upper-intermediate level. An immersion experience that includes home stays with local families, the course emphasizes the multicultural aspects of this region and facilitates student interaction with the local population. Field trips, events, lectures and language instruction are conducted by experts on the campus of the Universit√® des Antilles. Prerequisite: French 231 or higher (or equivalent) or placement in French 232 or higher. Open to first-year students.
