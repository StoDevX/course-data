BIO 231: Microbiology (2013-2014, Semester 2)

Microbiology examines the morphology, composition, metabolism, and genetics of microorganisms with emphasis on bacteria and viruses. Students examine the dynamic impact of microbes on humans, the immune response, and the role of microbes in the environment. Students attend lectures plus one three-hour laboratory per week. Prerequisites: Biology 143 and one Chemistry course, or Biology 227, or Biology 125 and Chemistry 121 or 125, or Chemistry/Biology 125-227. Offered annually.
