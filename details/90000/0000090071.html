NEURO 239: Cellular and Molecular Neuroscience (2013-2014, Semester 2)

Neuroscience is one of the fastest-growing fields in the sciences, with research interests ranging from molecular genetics to whole animal behavior. Topics include membrane biophysics, synaptic transmission and plasticity, intracellular signaling, sensory transduction, motor control systems, and development. Counts towards the biology and psychology majors. Prerequisite: Biology 125 or Integrated Chemistry/Biology 227 or Biology 227 or Psychology 238. Offered annually, usually in the spring semester.
