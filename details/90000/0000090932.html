MUSIC 282: Vocal Solo Literature II (0.25) (2013-2014, Semester 2)

Students survey the solo art song repertoire of France, England, the United States, and the Nordic countries. This course highlights the significant features and development of the art song, and provides context, approaches, and resources for studying this literature. Course work includes in-class performance. Prerequisite: Music 214, 241, 242, or permission of instructor. Preferred prerequisite: Music 263, 264. Offered spring semester 2013-14 and alternate years.
