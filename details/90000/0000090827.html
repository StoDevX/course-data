EDUC 391C: Student Teaching: Additional License (2013-2014, Semester 2)

Students teaching provides the best possible introduction to the teaching experience. Student teachers practice all classroom teaching skills required to assure competency to begin their first year of teaching. Students are assigned to a full-time internship in schools under the guidance of a competent cooperating teacher and supervised by faculty from both the content area department and the Education Department. Minimum of 6-10 full-time weeks teaching. Only student teachers can enroll: seniors or college graduates seeking an additional license. Offered each semester and Interim. Cannot be taken concurrently with Education 389 or 390. Prerequisite: completion of Education 389 or 390 or equivalent.
