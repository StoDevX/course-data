ART 104: Foundation New Media (2013-2014, Semester 2)

This foundation-level studio course introduces the aesthetic, conceptual, and technical foundations of new media art-making and the ways new media inform the creation and understanding of art in cultural contexts. Students explore the form and space of new media with an emphasis on time-based, interactive, and digital formats. The course emphasizes strategies for idea generation. Students engage in dynamic activity, spirited investigation, and thoughtful creative expression. Materials fee. Offered each semester. Counts toward film studies concentration.
