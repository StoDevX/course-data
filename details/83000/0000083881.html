ART 233: Advanced Two-Dimensional Studio (2012-2013, Interim)

This course, designed for students who want to heighten their two-dimensional skills, emphasizes using expressive qualities of art making by investigating various media, techniques, and content. Assignments are both traditional and nontraditional within historical and contemporary perspectives. Experimentation is encouraged. Students are encouraged to develop a personal visual language. The course culminates in the production of a series of works that relate thematically. Critical thinking and discussion skills are important. Prerequisite: Art 102. Materials fee. Offered annually.
