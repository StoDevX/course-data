ART 229: Video Art (2013-2014, Semester 1)

This course focuses on the creative use of digital video. Students study all aspects of video production from concept to screening, including idea generation, pre-production planning, storyboarding, lighting, shooting, editing, and sound design. Additionally, students regularly screen, analyze, and discuss contemporary and historic video art and avant-garde films. Students produce short video projects with special emphasis on conceptual thinking and experimentation. Prerequisites: Art 104 or permission of instructor. Materials fee. Offered alternate years. Counts toward film studies and media studies concentrations.
