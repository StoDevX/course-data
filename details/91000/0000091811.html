MUSIC 167: Percussion Techniques (0.25) (2013-2014, Semester 1)

Students learn basic performance practice on core instruments in each instrument family. Additional topics include: common performance issues and their possible solution; Best Practices in teaching and learning core instruments of the family; and technology for teaching, learning, or composing for instruments as appropriate. Prerequisite: permission of instructor required for students not majoring in theory/composition or music education. Offered fall semester 2013-14; thereafter annually in the spring semester.
