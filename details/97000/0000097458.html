MUSEN 101: Collegiate Chorale (0) (2014-2015, Semester 1)

This non-auditioned women's ensemble is open to students in all four class years as well as faculty, staff and emerita. Emphasis is on a less-demanding rehearsal schedule to create an enjoyable choral experience for all members. The ensemble conductor provides full details of all rehearsal and performance obligations. P/N only. May be repeated.
