GERM 234: German in Vienna (abroad) (2014-2015, Interim)

An immersion experience for students at the intermediate level or above, this course combines morning language classes with afternoon excursions, work with Austrian language partners, and service learning. Each week, students study a different topic of everyday Austrian culture, such as immigration and multiculturalism, social and religious groups, political parties, and Vienna as a music capital. Taught in German. Prerequisite: GERM 231 or equivalent. Counts toward German major and German studies concentration. Offered during Interim.
