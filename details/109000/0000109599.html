GERM 233: Language and Culture Studies in Germany (abroad) (2016-2017, Interim)

A total immersion experience and capstone course for students completing the language requirement. This course integrates listening, speaking, reading, writing, and cultural competence by exploring the recent history and contemporary aspects of major German cities, especially Berlin. Students self-select immersion situations, keep a journal of field experiences, and write short papers on topics from journal notes. Taught in German. May be counted toward the German major or German studies concentration. Offered periodically during Interim. Open to first-year students. Prerequisite: GERM 231 or equivalent.
