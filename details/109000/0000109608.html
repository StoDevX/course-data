MUSIC 225: Music in the Electronic Medium (2016-2017, Interim)

This course is a hands-on study of the science, aesthetics, history and current practices of electronic music. Creative projects include work with MIDI synthesizers and sequencers, as well as digital sampling and software synthesis. Offered during Interim 2016-2017 and alternate years. Counts toward media studies concentration. Prerequisite: sophomore standing or above.
