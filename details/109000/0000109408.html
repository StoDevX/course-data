PHYS 232A: Analytical Physics III (2016-2017, Semester 1)

Physics 232, the third course in the three-semester calculus-based sequence, explores special relativity, waves and oscillations, and the quantum mechanics of light and matter. There is one 2.5 hour laboratory meeting per week. Offered annually in the fall semester. Prerequisites: PHYS 131 or permission of instructor, and concurrent registration in (or previous completion of) MATH 220.
