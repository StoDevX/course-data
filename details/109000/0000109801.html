MUSEN 171: Chamber Music (0 credit) (2016-2017, Semester 1)

Through regular rehearsal and coaching sessions, new or already-formed chamber groups prepare and perform selected literature, and learn about related repertoire while cultivating observation, communication, and leadership skills. Typical ensembles would include instrumentalists and/or vocalists, with 3 to 8 performers, one on a part. Examples would be string quarters, brass or woodwind quintets, piano trios, saxophone quartets, and jazz combos. May be repeated. Offered each semester. Prerequisite: permission of instructor.
