PSCI 296: Political Science Research Practicum (2016-2017, Semester 1)

The political science research practicum provides a new opportunity to realize the college and department's commitment to undergraduate research. Students will put research skills into practive by gathering and analyzing empirical evidence, and producing a substantive research project. With research topics varying from term to term, students will develop methodological expertise surrounding a current problem in political science, and will collect and analyze data to address the problem. Offered alternate years.
