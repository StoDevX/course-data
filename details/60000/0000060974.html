GERM 372: Topics in German Studies (2007-2008, Semester 2)

Students explore a particular topic in language, literature, history, or culture through close reading, discussion, analysis, and interpretation of selected works, including theoretical texts. Sample topics include: Kafkas Europe, the Faust epic, women's voices, German heritage, the German holocaust, and Germany in Europe. Taught in German. Prerequisite: minimum of one 270-level course. May be repeated if topic is different.
