PSCI 284: Ethics of Citizenship in Times of War and Terrorism (2007-2008, Semester 2)

This course examines Just War theory and Jihad to enquire about the connections between religions and political violence. It considers the strategic logic of terrorism and the ethical "costs" of responses to it. The course also studies conflicts between "moderates" and "extremists" within Islam in order to reveal the ethical and political spectrums within it. Offered annually. Required prior experience of political analysis or the study of religion.
