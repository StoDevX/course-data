EDUC 377: New York City Education Seminar and Practicum (2007-2008, Interim)

Students spend sixteen days working as teaching assistants in a New York City school and four days observing various innovative Manhattan schools. Through readings, guest speakers, and hands-on experience, students will learn about the challenges and rewards of working in a diverse school environment. Prerequisites: Education 290, 2.50 GPA, and satisfactory field placement experience. Fulfills Education 382 and partially fulfills field experience requirement. Students apply in the Spring Semester. Counts toward the major: American Racial and Multicultural Studies. P/N only. Offered during alternate Interims.
