HIST 226: Modern France (2011-2012, Semester 1)

This course surveys the history of France since 1774, tracing elements of stability and upheaval from the Old Regime to the Fifth Republic. Emphasis is on the revolutionary tradition and the development of republicanism, social history of the 19th century, de Gaulle and his legacy, the Socialist experiment, and contemporary challenges facing a pluralistic society within a unitary state tradition. Foreign Language Across the Curriculum course available in French. Offered most years.
