ART 239: Video Art Production (2011-2012, Interim)

This course explores video as a creative medium. Students learn basic video production and editing techniques, producing a number of individual and group projects. During the semester students examine a wide variety of videos and films, exploring experimental, documentary, and narrative approaches. The class also considers the history of video art and its relationship to other time-based and static art forms. Prerequisite: Art 104 or Art 117 or permission of the instructor. Materials fee. Offered alternate years. Counts toward film studies concentration.
