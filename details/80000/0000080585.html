ENVST 283: Topics with ALS-L (2011-2012, Interim)

Sample Topic: Literature of the Poles

The earth's polar regions are today experiencing the greatest environmental change anywhere on earth. This course explores the historical, literary, and artistic works from places of great beauty and natural extremes: accounts from the turn-of-the-century heroic expeditions; early- to mid-20th-century photographers, writers, and scientists; and the works of contemporary artists photographers, writers, film makers, and scientists who depict conditions and change in the polar regions today.
