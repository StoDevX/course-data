ENGL 281: Topics in Genre (WRI only) (2016-2017, Interim)

This course focuses on the study and practice of literary publishing. Students explore the modern history and trends of publishing in America, as well as engage with written, oral, and digital projects that converse with readings and offer practical skills for students interested in publishing as either writers or editors. Projects may include the drafting and revision of a proposal for a hypothetical magazine, a book review, and a piece of digital fiction or marketing. Prerequisite: FYW.
