CHEM 374L: Advanced Synthesis Laboratory (0.25) (2016-2017, Semester 1)

Building upon skills learned in CHEM 253 and CHEM 254, students perform multi-step syntheses that require the use of advanced techniques for the synthesis, purification and characterization of organic compounds. The second half of the course may focus on a project chosen by each student in consultation with the instructor. P/N only. Offered annually in the spring semester. Prerequisites: CHEM 253 and CHEM 254.
