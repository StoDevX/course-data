ART 275: Topics in Art History (2016-2017, Semester 2)

The museum field is broad and inherently interdisciplinary, incorporating a range of topics including artistic, cultural, ethical, financial and legal questions. In this seminar course, students will explore these issues and more in addition to learning about the practicalities of a range of museum departments and jobs (including collections management and care, curation,development, and education). Students will also work with the Flaten Museum's collection.
