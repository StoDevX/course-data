MATH 117: Gateways to Mathematics (2009-2010, Summer Session 1)

This course explores the nature of mathematics and its role in the modern world. The content and format of the course vary, depending on the instructor. The course may focus on one topic or survey a range of topics. Recent topics include environmental modeling, social choice, routes and networks, scheduling, and symmetry. The course is intended for all students. Offered each semester.
