DNCTE 220: (0.50), 221 (0.25) Ballet II (2005-2006, Semester 2)

This course offers an expansion of ballet vocabulary and technique with an emphasis on movement quality, integrity and efficiency. Outside work includes practicing, reading, writing and concert attendance. . Prerequisite: Dance 121 (at least twice) and/or permission of the instructor. Offered both semesters. 221 can be repeated with instructors permission.
