REL 275: Historical Geography and the Bible (abroad) (2005-2006, Interim)

This course concentrates on the historical and geographical background of the Bible. Visits to archaeological sites in Galilee, Judea and Jerusalem especially enable students to understand the religious pluralism of ancient Palestine as well as the Jewish roots of Christianity. A secondary emphasis of the course is on the current diversity of worship and piety in Jerusalem. Offered in Interim. Offered in 2002-03.
