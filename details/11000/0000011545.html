SPAN 234: Intermediate Spanish II in Costa Rica (abroad) (2005-2006, Interim)

Students explore geographic, economic, political and ethnic dimensions of life in present-day Costa Rica by combining intensive course work with such cross-cultural experiences as a homestay in San Jose; field work, visits to sites of cultural interest and excursions to the geographically and culturally diverse Atlantic and Pacific coasts. They consolidate their language skills and continue to develop their skills for analyzing and communicating in Spanish by writing compositions, making oral presentations, interviewing their host families and engaging in other interactive group activities. Prerequisite: Spanish 231 or placement.
