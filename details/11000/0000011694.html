ART 230: Motion Graphics (2005-2006, Semester 2)

This course explores the computer as a tool for creating visual images with movement and sound. Emphasizing concept development and creative expression, students investigate time-based software, including animation and video capture programs. The course introduces students to the work of traditional and computer animators. Prerequisites: Art 111 and 112 (or 120), or 115, or permission of the instructor. Lab fee. Offered annually.
