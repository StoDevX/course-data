ID 239: Peacebuilding and conflict management (2005-2006, Interim)

Peacebuilding is a growing field that engages international organizations and governments as well as civil society organizations and community activists. This course provides an introduction to the field of peacebuilding and conflict management and an opportunity to apply this knowledge to current events through case studies and practice in conflict management skills. Topics covered include cultures of peace, the role of history and future in conflict, conflict analysis and dynamics, intervention strategies, identity and reconciliation. Offered during Interim.
