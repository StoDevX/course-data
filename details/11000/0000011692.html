ART 224: Sculpture/Direct Metal (2005-2006, Semester 2)

This sculpture course introduces students to metal froming, shaping, fastening and brazing and welding, uilding upon concepts from the foundationcourse and presenting the next level of sculpture topics, issues and concerns. Both majors and non-majors discover an artand industrial process that has great artistic and practical application The course makes the use of hands-on instruction, readings, slide talks and discussion. Lab fee. Prerequisite: Art 114 (Mandatory); Art 111, 112, 113, 116(recommended). Offered annually. Spring Semester only.
