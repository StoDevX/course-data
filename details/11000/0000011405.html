GERM 245: Arts in Vienna (abroad; in English) (2005-2006, Interim)

Explores Viennas cultural traditions as revealed in museums, theaters, operas, operettas, concerts and the citys architectural masterpieces. Focus on turn-of-the-century Vienna and the shift from the late-19th-century romanticism of the declining Habsburg Empire to an unprecedented modernism in all the arts, including architecture (Loos) and music (Schonberg), as well as psychology (Freud), science (Mach) and philosophy (Wittgenstein). Taught in English. GE: ALS-A, HWC. Offered alternate Interims.
