GERM 233: Language and Culture Studies in Germany (Abroad) (2005-2006, Interim)

A total immersion experience and capstone course for students completing the language requirement. Integrates listening, speaking, reading, writing and cultural competency. Exploration of city culture, recent history and contemporary aspects of three major German cities: Cologne, Bonn and Berlin. Meetings with German students and professors, museum visits, performances, intensive daily classes and exploration of individually chosen topics. Taught in German. Prerequisite: German 232, B grade or higher in 231, or equivalent. Open to first-year students. Fulfills the language requirement and can be counted toward the German major. GE: FOL-G. Offered every Interim.
