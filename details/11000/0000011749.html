AMCON 202A: Pursuits of Happiness, 1920-2000 (2005-2006, Semester 2)

Students in this course examine technology, the mass market and consumerism, and the increasingly complex relation between identity and material goods. They will also explore the images, institutions and stories of enviornmental, feminist and Civil War culture. Topics and texts include Yosemite national Park, Japenese internment camps, Adrienne Rich's poetry and prose, Freedom Summer, Las Vegas and the Mall of America.
