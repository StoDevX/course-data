CLASS 243: The Golden Age of Greece (2005-2006, Semester 2)

Students journey back to the 5th century B.C., as the Athenians emerge triumphant from the Persian Wars and develop the "Golden Age of Greece. Studying the history, literature and art of ancient Athens helps students understand the uniqueness of that city-state and its lasting contributions to Western civilization. GE: HWC, ALS-L. Offered in alternate years.
