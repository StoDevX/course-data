EDUC 231: Drugs and Alcohol (0) (2005-2006, Semester 2)

Students examine issues related to chemical use/abuse in our society. The course, required of all candidates for a teaching license, enhances problem identification and helping skills in students' professional and personal lives. Students develop an understanding of enabling and intervening behaviors and gain knowledge about the role of schools in addressing these and other chemical abuse concerns. Prerequisite: at least junior standing. Offered both semesters, evenings. P/N only.
