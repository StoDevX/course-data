MUSIC 251A: Conducting (0.50) (2014-2015, Semester 2)

Students learn basic conducting gestures (with and without baton) through exercises in meter patterns, preparatory beats and cut-offs, cueing, dynamics, fermata, articulations, phrasing, left hand independence, and face/eye usage. Offered annually in the spring semester. Prerequisite: MUSIC 114 or permission of instructor.
