GREEK 231: Intermediate Greek (2016-2017, Semester 1)

Third-semester Greek students translate selections from Plato's dialogues (Apology, Crito, Phaedo) while reviewing vocabulary, grammar, and syntax. Topics for class discussion include the life and death of Socrates and the significance of the dialogues as works of literature. Offered annually in the fall semester. Counts toward linguistic studies concentration. Prerequisite: GREEK 112 or equivalent.
