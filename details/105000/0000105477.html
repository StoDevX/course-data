NURS 312: Family Health (2016-2017, Semester 1)

Students focus on the nursing care of childbearing and childrearing families in the context of family-centered care. Family theory will be utilized as the framework for nursing assessment and care of families in various situations across the lifespan. Clinical experiences are provided with childbearing and childrearing families. Counts toward the nursing major. Offered annually in the fall semester. Prerequisite: open to junior nursing majors only; NURS 211; concurrent registration in NURS 311 and NURS 313.
