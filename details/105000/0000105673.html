SPAN 231G: Intermediate Spanish I (2016-2017, Semester 1)

Through exploring the geographic and human diversity of the Spanish-speaking world, students develop increasingly complex skills for analyzing and communicating in Spanish. They study such essential dimensions of that world as: (1) geography and development; (2) environmental challenges and solutions; (3) population and demographic changes and challenges; and (4) ethnic diversity. Offered each semester and during Interim. Prerequisite: SPAN 112 or placement.
