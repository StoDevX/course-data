ESTH 374: Biomechanics (2016-2017, Semester 1)

Students analyze mechanical principles in depth as they affect human motion. Topics include study of muscular and skeletal systems, skill analysis, and motion measurement techniques.The course includes a laboratory component. Counts toward biomedical studies (for students through class of 2016) and neuroscience concentrations. Offered annually. Prerequisite: BIO 143 and junior standing.
