CHEM 374L: Advanced Synthesis Laboratory (0.25) (2012-2013, Semester 2)

Building upon skills learned in Chemistry 253, 254, students perform multi-step syntheses that require the use of advanced techniques for the synthesis, purification and characterization of organic compounds. The second half of the course may focus on a project chosen by each student in consultation with the instructor. P/N only. Prerequisites: Chemistry 253, 254. Offered annually in the spring semester.
