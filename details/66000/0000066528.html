MUSIC 132: Orchestral Music (2008-2009, Interim)

In this course, students explore orchestral music in history, forms, styles, instrumentation, vocabulary, and specific landmark works. The course includes attendance of four orchestral concerts/rehearsals in Minneapolis/St. Paul. These concerts will likely be scheduled outside of the lecture times, for example Thursday or Friday evenings. Estimated cost: $100. Offered Interim only.
