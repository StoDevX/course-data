GERM 111B: Beginning German I (2009-2010, Semester 1)

Students begin to learn German through listening, speaking, reading, and writing about situations familiar to them including their personal biographies, family, daily life, studies, travels, and hobbies. Regular writing assignments are designed to learn vocabulary, check spelling, and to form thoughts with German sentence structure. Regular language lab activities aid in acquiring good pronunciation and listening skills. Offered annually in the Fall Semester.
