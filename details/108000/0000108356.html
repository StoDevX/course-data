MUSIC 242A: History And Literature of Music II (2016-2017, Semester 2)

Continuing the study begun in MUSIC 241, students encounter the history and development of Western European music from ca. 1750 to the present and study the major forms, styles and representative literature of the Classic and Romantic eras and the 20th and 21st centuries. Offered annually in the spring semester. Prerequisite: MUSIC 114.
