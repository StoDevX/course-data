BIO 231: Microbiology (2016-2017, Semester 2)

Microbiology examines the morphology, composition, metabolism, and genetics of micro organisms with emphasis on bacteria and viruses. Students examine the dynamic impact of microbes on humans, the immune response, and the role of microbes in the environment. Students attend lectures plus one three-hour laboratory per week. Offered annually. Prerequisites: BIO 143 or BIO 150, and one Chemistry course.
