JAPAN 112A: Beginning Japanese II (2016-2017, Semester 2)

This course is an introduction to speaking, reading, and writing Japanese; writing includes the learning of all syllabic letters (Hiragana and Katakana) and basic Kanji (Chinese characters). Class meets four times weekly. Individual language laboratory visits are also required. Offered annually in the spring semester. Prerequisite: JAPAN 111 or equivalent.
