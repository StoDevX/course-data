MUSEN 106: St. Olaf Choir (0) (2016-2017, Semester 2)

The college's touring choir of 75 mixed voices continues its original mission of primarily focusing on a wide range of sacred choral literature. Participation is by audition and does not include first-year students. The ensemble conductor provides full details of all rehearsal and performance obligations. P/N only. May be repeated. Students in the St. Olaf Choir are required to register for voice lessons.
