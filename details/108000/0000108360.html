NURS 211: Holistic Health Assessment and Health Promotion (2016-2017, Semester 2)

This course focuses on holistic health assessment, health promotion, and disease prevention across the lifespan. Students obtain health histories, identify risk factors, and develop health assessment skills in physiologic, psychosocial, developmental, and spiritual dimensions. Identification of data from a holistic perspective and analysis of findings through critical thinking are emphasized. Active learning experiences are utilized to apply knowledge and informatics during health assessment. Counts toward the nursing major. Offered annually in the spring semester. Prerequisites: open to sophomore nursing majors only; CNA certification; concurrent registration in ID 210.
