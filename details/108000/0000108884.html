MUSEN 122: St. Olaf Orchestra (0) (2016-2017, Semester 2)

The college's touring symphony orchestra of approximately 90-95 instrumentalists performs a broad range of repertoire. Participation is by audition. The ensemble conductor provides full details of all rehearsal and performance obligations. P/N only. May be repeated. Students in the St. Olaf Orchestra are required to register for lessons on their orchestral instrument.
