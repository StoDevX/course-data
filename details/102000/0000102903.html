BIO 348: Research in Electron Microscopy (off campus) (2015-2016, Interim)

This course introduces students to intensive research at St. Olaf and the Boulder Laboratory for 3-D Electron Microscopy of Cells at the University of Colorado. In Boulder, students prepare samples for electron microscopy and immuno-gold Electron Microscopy, capture EM-images, and generate 3-D Tomograms. At St. Olaf students generate 3-D computer models of their datasets. Offered occasionally during Interim. Prerequisite: three courses in biology or permission of instructor.
