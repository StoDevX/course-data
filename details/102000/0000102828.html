MUSIC 343: Music Of The Classical And Romantic Eras (2015-2016, Semester 1)

Intensive study of musical literature between 1750 and 1900. The course begins with the development of the Viennese Classical School (Haydn, Mozart, Beethoven) and continues with European trends in Romantic opera, Lieder, symphony and chamber music (from Rossini through R. Strauss). Offered fall semester 2015-16 and alternate years. Counts toward German studies concentration. Prerequisites: MUSIC 241 and MUSIC 242.
