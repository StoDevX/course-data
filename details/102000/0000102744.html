ASIAN 156: Contemporary China Through Film (in English translation) (2015-2016, Semester 1)

This course examines contemporary China through Chinese language cinema across different eras, genres, filmmakers, and geographic regions. Students will explore the art of film and how visual narratives represent, reinterpret, and redefine Chinese society and culture both at home and abroad. Discussion topics include modernity and tradition, nationalism and globalization, class and race, gender and sexuality, independent and commercial films. All readings are in English. Counts toward film studies concentration. FLAC component available.
