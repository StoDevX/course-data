CHEM 254AL: Synthesis Laboratory II (0.25) (2015-2016, Semester 2)

This course is a continuation of CHEM 253. Students gain more experience with techniques used in CHEM 253 and in addition use gas chromatographic/mass spectrometric analyses. Students attend one three-hour laboratory each week. P/N only. Offered annually in the spring semester. Counts toward environmental studies major (natural science emphasis) and biomolecular science concentration. Prerequisites: CHEM 253 and previous or concurrent registration in CHEM 248.
