CSCI 350: Advanced Team Project (2015-2016, Interim)

This course is devoted to team research/development projects, employing established software development methodologies. Projects frequently have an interdisciplinary nature, involving consultation with faculty and/or students in other fields, and taking advantage of the particular backgrounds of team members. One or more research papers, posters ,etc., on results are ordinarily expected. Offered alternate years. Prerequisites: one core course with implementation in computer science or permission of instructor.
