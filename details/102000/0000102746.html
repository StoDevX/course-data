CHIN 351: Chinese Language and Society through the Media (2015-2016, Semester 1)

This advanced Chinese language course aims to develop students' language proficiency and introduce students to various aspects of contemporary Chinese social life and culture. Course materials include films with excerpts of written scripts, newspapers, television, and essays related to theunit topics. Classroom activities include lectures, language drills, discussions, debates, presentations, and performances. Taught in Chinese. Prerequisite: CHIN 302 or permission of instructor.
