AS/PS 255: Asian Citizenships: Identities and Rights (2015-2016, Semester 2)

How do people in Asia understand citizenship? Students learn how membership in cultural, social, and political communities shapes rights, responsibilities, and identities in Asian countries. Reading historical and social science research, students consider and compare citizenship in Asian countries, including China, Japan, Korea, Taiwan, and Vietnam. Offered periodically. Prerequisite: one previous course in Asian studies or political science.
