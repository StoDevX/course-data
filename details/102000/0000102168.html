EDUC 355: Teaching of Music in the Elementary School (2015-2016, Semester 2)

The goal of the course is to acquire the knowledge, skills, and understandings necessary to develop, implement and assess a K-5 general music program. The course emphasizes current approaches such as those of Orff, Dalcroze and Kodaly as well as intercultural and interdisciplinary learning. Required field experience component. Offered annually in the spring semester. Prerequisite: EDUC 330.
