PSYCH 249: Social Psychology (2014-2015, Summer Session 1)

Why are people prejudiced and how can we reduce prejudice? Why do people help others? What is self-esteem and how do we defend it? How does romantic attraction develop? What are emotions and how do they influence us? In this introduction to the ways people interact and think about each other, students design their own theories of social behavior. Offered annually. Prerequisite: PSYCH 125.
