NURS 306: Health Assessment (2015-2016, Semester 1)

Classroom and laboratory experiences in nursing and health assessment skills develop the student's ability to provide nursing care. Opportunities are provided to apply knowledge from the liberal arts and nursing theory to simulated patient care situations. Individual practice sessions in the nursing laboratory are expected. Performance testing determines readiness for the role of caregiver in a variety of clinical settings. Offered annually in the fall semester. Prerequisites: concurrent registration in NURS 302, NURS 304, and NURS 308.
