RUSSN 254: Russian Culture and Civilization (2017-2018, Semester 2)

This survey of Russian culture and civilization uses original Russian texts on history, the arts, religion, and education, as well as documentary films and selections from literature. The course fosters the acquisition of the language of Russian cultural and intellectual discourse. Offered annually in the spring semester. Counts toward management studies concentration. Prerequisite: RUSSN 251 or permission of the instructor.
