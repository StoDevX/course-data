SWRK 396: Directed Undergraduate Research (2017-2018, Semester 2)

This course provides a research opportunity for students with interests in end of life care and decision-making. We will examine the multidisciplinary literature, design a pilot project and collect data. Open to seniors only; Prerequisite: research methods course in social work, sociology, anthropology or psychology. Registration by application/permission of instructor. Meeting days and times will be determined as part of the application process for the course. Closed during web registration.
