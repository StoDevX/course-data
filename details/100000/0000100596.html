MUSIC 364: Vocal Pedagogy (0.25) (2015-2016, Semester 1)

This course introduces a systematic pedagogical approach to the study of voice production for use in the studio or classroom. Students examine basic voice physiology, the healthy use and care of the voice, voice problems, and appropriate vocal literature. Offered annually in the fall semester. Counts toward management studies concentration. Prerequisite: three semesters of voice lessons, one of which may be voice class; or permission of instructor.
