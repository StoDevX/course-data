THEAT 250: Lighting and Sound-Design and Production (2015-2016, Semester 1)

The design and production of lighting and sound in theater, dance, music, and general presentation work plays a significant role in the experience of these events. This course introduces the production problems of lighting and sound and includes design projects in both lighting and sound. Course fee required. Open to all majors and non-majors. Offered annually in the fall semester. Prerequisite: THEAT 140 is recommended.
