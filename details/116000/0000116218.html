CHEM 297H: Independent Research (0.25, 0.50, 1.00) (2017-2018, Semester 1)

Independent research is offered for students dedicated to an in-depth research experience. Emphasis is placed on the iterative process of experimentation and analysis. Students interested in independent research may enroll in CHEM 297 or CHEM 398. Each course requires a faculty supervisor, who will make the decision as to which course is appropriate. This course can only be taken Pass or No Pass (P/N) and not for a grade. Offered each term. Prerequisite: permission of the instructor.
