DANCE 250: Modern Dance II: Technique Emphasis (.50) (2014-2015, Semester 2)

Students further develop artistic, athletic technical skills in modern dance through concentrated work on movement sequencing and dance conditioning. Students learn, create, and perform movement patterns rich in attention toward actions and alignments of the spine, spatial pathways, dynamics of time and energy, expression of weight/gravity, and intricate foot and legwork. Offered spring semester. Prerequisite: two terms of DANCE 111 or permission of instructor. Open to dance majors and other students with permission of instructor.
