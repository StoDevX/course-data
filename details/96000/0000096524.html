EDUC 364: Teaching of Science, 5-12 (2014-2015, Semester 2)

Prospective science teachers learn strategies for the practical application of learning theory to secondary school science classrooms. Topics include: the national science standards movement and No Child Left Behind; inquiry learning in the science classroom; lesson planning; teaching in the classroom and in the laboratory; technology: managing a science laboratory: and continuing professional development. Required field experience component. Offered annually in the spring semester. Prerequisite: EDUC 330.
