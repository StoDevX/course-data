ESTH 375: Physiology of Exercise (1.0) (2014-2015, Semester 2)

Students study in-depth the physiology of exercise, covering cardiovascular and muscular adaptions to exercise and factors affecting performance, including body composition, environmental influences, training implications across gender and age, and the assessment of fitness. The course includes a laboratory component. Offered annually. Prerequisite: junior standing and BIO 143 and BIO 243 or consent of instructor.
