CHEM 390: The Literature of Chemistry (0.25) (2014-2015, Semester 2)

Students explore the literature of chemistry in a seminar setting. Experience is gained inreading, discussing, and presenting chemistry-related results from the current scientific literature. Each section has a distinct topical focus selected by the chemistry faculty from suggestions made by junior chemistry majors during the spring of the previous year. P/N only. Open to senior chemistry majors only. Offered annually in the spring semester.
