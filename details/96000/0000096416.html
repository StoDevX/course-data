THEAT 240: Scenery and Costume Design and Production (2014-2015, Semester 2)

This course balances the elements of production with the elements of design, focusing on materials, practices, and techniques used to create scenic and costume elements used in live production. The course includes hands-on experience in the scenic and costume studios, and projects in technical drawing, designing, analysis, and research. A course fee is required. Open to majors and non-majors. Offered annually in the spring semester. Prequisite: THEAT 140 is recommended.
