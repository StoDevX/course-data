EDUC 321: Teaching of Reading, 5-12 (0.50) (2014-2015, Semester 2)

This course provides a theoretical and practical foundation for assisting secondary readers. Students learn the essentials of good reading instruction and apply them in the making of a three-week literature unit. Required 10-hour field experience. Offered annually in the spring semester. Prerequisites: EDUC 290 and EDUC 330, or permission of instructor.
