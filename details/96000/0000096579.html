MEDIA 360: Topics in Media (2014-2015, Semester 2)

This course provides a capstone to the concentration. Taught as a seminar, it investigates critical and specialized issues in media from multiple and often competing perspectives. Topics change regularly and address a wide range of media-related concerns. Sample topics include Media and the Environment, Media and Religion, Media and Globalization. Offered annually in the spring semester. Prerequisite: MEDIA 160 or permission of instructor.
