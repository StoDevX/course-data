ASIAN 215: Asian Conversations II: Encountering Asia (abroad) (2017-2018, Interim)

Students pursue guided fieldwork experience in China and Japan. Activities and readings in this course build ong the topics from from ASIAN 210 and three semesters of language study. Students continue to explore their understanding of Asia through ethnographic observation, interviews, and site visits. Students develop projects and follow a process of inquiry that will help them understand how ordinary people construct "Asian" culture and society today. Offered during Interim. Prerequisite: ASIAN 210.
