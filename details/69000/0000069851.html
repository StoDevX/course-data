MUSIC 314: Analysis of 20th- and 21st-Century Music (2009-2010, Semester 2)

An analytical study of the wide range of musical styles found in the 20th- and 21st-centuries, this course provides an opportunity to study important modern works and to come to a deeper understanding of their structure and meaning. Students study atonal music theory in depth and examine some of the new approaches to tonality in our time. Prerequisite: Music 214. Offered Spring Semester 2009-10 and alternate years.
