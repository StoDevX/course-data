MEDIA 360: Topics in Media (2009-2010, Semester 2)

This course provides a capstone to the concentration. Taught as a seminar, it investigates critical and specialized issues in media from multiple and often competing perspectives. Topics change regularly and address a wide range of media-related concerns. Sample topics include Media and the Environment, Media and Religion, Media and Child Development, and Documentary Studies. Prerequisite: Media Studies 160 or permission of instructor. Offered annually in the Spring Semester.
