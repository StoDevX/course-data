DANCE 210A: Modern Dance II (0.50) (2009-2010, Semester 2)

Students expand their understanding of modern dance by building on the components of Dance 111, adding attention to movement qualities, elements of space, dance vocabulary and of technique with an emphasis on phrasing and sequencing movement. Open to majors and other students with instructor's permission. Prerequisite: Dance 111 and permission of instructor. Offered each semester.
