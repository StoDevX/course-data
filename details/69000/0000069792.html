CHEM 107: Forensic Science with Lab (2009-2010, Semester 2)

In addition to the in-class experience shared with Chemistry 106 and described above, this course offers a laboratory component with experiments that feature the use of forensic techniques to collect and analyze evidence including fingerprinting, drug analysis, alcohol investigation, DNA fingerprinting, and fiber analysis. Students attend three classes and one 3-hour laboratory per week. Offered alternate years in the Spring Semester.
