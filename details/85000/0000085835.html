AMST 209: American Studies: A Topical Approach (2012-2013, Semester 1)

Students apply theories and methods of culture studies to a selected major topic in American culture. The course employs a discussion format, focusing on critical reading, analytic essays. Recent topics include "Masculinity in America," "American Adolescence," and "Campus Ecology." Usually offered annually in the spring semester. May be repeated if topics are different.
