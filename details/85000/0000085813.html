SPAN 270: Spain's Cultural and Linguistic Legacy (Abroad) (2012-2013, Interim)

This topics course explores a Spanish peninsular cultural, literary, and/or linguistic theme from a base in Spain through analysis and discussion of texts, guest lectures, excursions to appropriate cultural sites, field research, and related experiential activities. Sample topics include: Christians, Jews and Muslims in Spain, and Spain's Autonomous Communities. Taught in Spanish. Prerequisite: Spanish 250.
