ENGL 282: Chekhov's Legacy: A Study of the Short Story (2011-2012, Semester 2)

Students examine Anton Chekhov's legacy by reading his work in translation and exploring his impact on the genre of the short story through the study of his literary descendants. Authors to be considered include James Joyce, Ernest Hemingway, Grace Paley, and Ray Carver. Students explore these authors through reading, discussion, and a variety of written assignments. Prerequisite: FYW or equivalent. Offered occasionally.
