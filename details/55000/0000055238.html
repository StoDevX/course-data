THEAT 270: History of Theater up to 1700 (2006-2007, Semester 1)

Examines theatrical activities, from theater's origins to 1700, from three interrelated perspectives 1) theater as social history, 2) theater as dramatic literature and 3) theater as performance; i.e. the result of creative decisions made by playwrights, actors, directors and designers. Prerequisite: Theatre 110. Offered in 2002-03 and alternate years.
