MATH 120E: Calculus I (2006-2007, Semester 1)

This course introduces differential and integral calculus of functions of a single real variable, including trigonometric, exponential and logarithmic functions. Derivatives and integrals are explored graphically, symbolically and numerically. Applications of the derivative are included. Prerequisite: Mathematics Placement Recommendation. Credit may be earned for either Mathematics 120 or 122, but not both.
