MUSIC 288: Choral Literature II (0.50) (2015-2016, Semester 2)

This course is a study of the larger forms of choral music from the Baroque to the present. Students study scores and recordings and discuss representative style features and characteristics, interpretation, and conducting problems. Offered spring semester 2015-16 and alternate years. Prerequisite: MUSIC 214, MUSIC 241, and MUSIC 242 and junior standing.
