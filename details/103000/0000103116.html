SOAN 296: Top: Urban Sociology (2015-2016, Semester 1)

Urban sociology investigates the complex interactions of human beings in cities. Among the topics include neighborhood formation, social stratification along lines of religion, class, and race/ethnicity, and city governance. Chicago will serve as an important case study for considering these urban dynamics. Students interested in public policy, social work, and economics will be particularly welcome in the course, along with anyone else interested in city life. Counts toward race and ethnic studies major and concentration.
