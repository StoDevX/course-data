ART 151: Introduction to Art History II (2010-2011, Semester 2)

This introduction to art history examines select European, Native American, Euro-American, and African artistic traditions from c. 1400 to the present. Through lectures, discussions, and other activities, students develop skills in visual analysis and interpretation. The course considers a variety of artistic media, including painting, sculpture, architecture, and textiles, as well as broader issues of aesthetics, function, and patronage. Offered annually.
