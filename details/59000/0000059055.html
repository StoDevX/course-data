EDUC 317: Mathematics for Elementary Classrooms (2006-2007, Summer Session 2)

This methods course provides pre-service teachers with content and methods of teaching the number system, mathematical concepts, and arithmetical operations in grades K-6, with focus on a problem-solving based program. Includes opportunities for additional understanding of mathematical content. Pre-service teachers develop skills to communicate carefully and accurately about mathematics and its applications. In addition, they use technology when appropriate to enhance the learning of mathematics.
