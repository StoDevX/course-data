EDUC 316: Science, Health, and Active Living for Elementary Classrooms (2006-2007, Summer Session 2)

This methods course provides pre-service teachers with methodology, curricular content, and assessment techniques that provide and promote meaningful learning of science and skills for lifelong healthy, active living by elementary school children. Pre-service teachers develop a personal vision of instruction that reflects the National Science Standards and the Minnesota Science Framework for teaching science to young children.
