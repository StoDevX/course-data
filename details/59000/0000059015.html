ENGL 257: Creative Writing I (2006-2007, Summer Session 1)

This course provides students with the opportunity to gather insights and develop skills in the writing of creative prose, poetry and/or drama. Literary selections are often used as models and discussions of craft set the stage for the workshopping of student writing. For more information on this course please refer to: http://www.stolaf.edu/depts/english/courses/index.html
