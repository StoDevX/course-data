EDUC 315: Reading, Communication, and Literacy Methods (2006-2007, Summer Session 1)

This methods course introduces pre-service teachers to theory, practice, and methods associated with developing reading/language art skills in elementary pupils. It includes an emphasis on teaching to pupils of diverse socio-economic backgrounds and cultures. This course continues the study of Education 322: Teaching of Reading K-6 and Education 321: Teaching of Reading 5-12.
