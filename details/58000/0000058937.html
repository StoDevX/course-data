MATH 364: Combinatorics (2007-2008, Semester 2)

This course covers basic enumeration, including generating functions, recursion, inclusion-exclusion, Polya theory, etc. Students also explore topics in graph theory and constructive combinatorics, time permitting. Prerequisite: Mathematics 252; some previous exposure to counting methods is helpful (e.g. Mathematics 262). Offered in 2007-08 and alternate years.
