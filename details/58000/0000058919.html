ENGL 283B: Studies in Prose (2007-2008, Semester 2)

Students explore various forms of prose, both fiction and nonfiction. Each instructor selects different material to be studied and emphasizes various aspects of the form. Topics can range from the 18th-century novel to African American autobiography. May be repeated if topics are different. Prerequisite: FYW or equivalent. For more information on this course please see the following website: http://www.stolaf.edu/depts/english/courses/
