BIO 251: Plant Physiology (2015-2016, Semester 2)

This course begins with an in-depth look at a plant cell and its physiology, followed by a discussion of whole plant physiology as it relates to cellular functions. Students attend lectures plus one three-hour laboratory per week. Counts as "comparative organismal biology" core category. Prerequisites: BIO 150 or BIO 126, CHEM 125 and CHEM 126; BIO 227 or CH/BI 227 recommended.
