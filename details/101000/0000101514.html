WRIT 111I: First-Year Writing (2015-2016, Semester 2)

THE NATURE OF NATURE WRITING. In the midst of climate change, extinction, and floating islands of garbage, what exactly is nature? How might writing about nature help to curtail the slow amble toward devastation around us? In this seminar, well think about the content of nature writing but more importantly about what kinds of writing strategies most effectively promote that content. The subjects well read about will be diverse: from definitions of sustainability to manifestos about hunting, from encounters with weasels to the absence of encounters with golden frogs, from the problems with consumption to the possibilities of cell phones as purveyors of green change. Assignments will include a personal narrative, a sustainability analysis essay, an editorial, and a radical re-creation project.
