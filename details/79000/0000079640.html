MUSIC 262B: Piano Class IV (0.25) (2011-2012, Semester 2)

This continuation of 261 emphasizes sight playing, score reading, harmonization and transposition, more advanced improvisation and harmonic usage, performance preparation, and leadership from the keyboard. A grade of B- or higher satisfies Piano Proficiency Level 4 requirements. Prerequisite: Music 261, completion of Piano Proficiency 3, or audition placement. Recommended co-requisite: Music 214. Offered annually in the spring semester.
