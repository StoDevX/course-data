REL 242: The Religious and the Political:Gandhi, Niebuhr, and King (2010-2011, Interim)

This course explores the interface between religion and politics in the lives and thought of Mahatma Gandhi, Reinhold Niebuhr, and Martin Luther King, Jr. Students focus on the distinctive ways in which each one appropriated the theological resources of his tradition to justify and analyze the connection between religion and politics. Prerequisite: BTS-B. Offered alternate interims.
