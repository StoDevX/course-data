THEAT 271: History of Theater since 1700 (2006-2007, Semester 1)

Examines theatrical activities, in the period 1700 to the present, from three interrelated perspectives 1) theater as social history, 2) theater as dramatic literature and 3) theater as performance; i.e. the result of creative decisions made by playwrights, actors, directors and designers. Prerequisite: Theatre 110. Offered in 2003-04 and alternative years.
