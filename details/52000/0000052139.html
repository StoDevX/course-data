MUSIC 261: Piano Class III (2006-2007, Semester 2)

This advanced piano class for B.M. music majors emphasizes sight playing, score reading, harmonization and transposition, more advanced harmonic idioms, improvisation, ensemble playing, and performance preparation. Students earning a grade of B- or higher meet the department's keyboard proficiency standards for B.M. instrumental performance majors. Prerequisite: Music 162, equivalency examination II, or audition placement.
