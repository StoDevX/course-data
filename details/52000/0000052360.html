PSYCH 390: Issues in Psychology (2006-2007, Semester 2)

"Issues courses" allow in-depth study of particular themes or topics in psychology. Students "get below the surface" of broad textbook summaries and introductory material. Past seminars have included: linguistics, personal narrative and lifestory, addictions, women and madness, integrative therapies and the biopsychology of mental disorders. Prerequisite: See department website. Specific topics are announced each year. May be repeated if topics are different.
