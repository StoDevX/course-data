EDUC 322: Teaching of Reading K-6 (0.5) (2006-2007, Semester 2)

This course, being offered for the last time in Spring 2007, provides students with the background needed to teach elementary reading. Students learn about whole language and skills-based instruction and examine how to integrate the two in a balance reading program. Required 10-hour field experience. Prerequisites: Education 290 and 330, or permission of instructor.
