MUSIC 344: Music of the 20th and 21st Centuries (2016-2017, Semester 2)

Intensive study of musical masterworks from ca.1890 to the present, focusing on formal and stylistic trends as well as the political, philosophical and economic contexts of Western art and popular music from French impressionism through American hip hop. Course work includes bibliographic instruction, lecture/discussion, and an analytic research paper. Offered spring semester 2016-2017 and alternate years. Prerequisites: MUSIC 241 and MUSIC 242.
