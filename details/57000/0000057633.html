PHYS 127: Analytical Physics II (2007-2008, Semester 2)

Physics 127, the second course in the three-semester calculus based sequence, explores special relativity, waves, atomic physics, heat, and thermodynamics. There is one 2.5-hour laboratory meeting per week. Prerequisites: Physics 126 or permission of instructor, and concurrent registration in (or previous completion of) Mathematics 126 or 128. Offered Spring Semester.
