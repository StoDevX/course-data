FREN 233: Language and Culture Studies in The French Caribbean (abroad) (2007-2008, Interim)

Students study the French language and French-Caribbean culture on the island of Martinique in an immersion experience that includes home stays. The course emphasizes the multicultural aspects of the region and facilitates student interaction with the local population.
