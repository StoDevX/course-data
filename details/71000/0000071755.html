MUSIC 234: Indian Music History and Performance (2009-2010, Interim)

Students experience the diverse musics of South Asia through the performance of Hindustani music, the classical music of North India and Pakistan. Topics will include bhajan, film music, various regional musics and qawwali, South Indian music, and changes in music institutions in the 20th century. No past musical training is required for this class, but all participants are expected to practice and perform together.
