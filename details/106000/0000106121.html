MUSEN 105: St. Olaf Chapel Choir (0) (2016-2017, Semester 1)

This choir of 120 mixed voices performs a wide range of choral repertoire that includes larger works for choir and orchestra. Participation is by audition and does not include first-year students. The ensemble conductor provides full details of all rehearsal and performance obligations. P/N only. May be repeated. Students in the St. Olaf Chapel Choir are encouraged to register for voice lessons.
