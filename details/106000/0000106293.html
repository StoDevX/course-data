FREN 251: Writing French (2016-2017, Semester 1)

Students engage in intensive practice in various types of writing in French (e.g., summary, extended description, narration, and professional correspondence). Literary and non-literary texts provide topics and models. The course involves discussion, writing, and revision, and stresses advanced grammar review. Taught in French. Counts toward management studies concentration. Prerequisite: FREN 232 or FREN 235, or equivalent.
