ART 103: Foundation Three-Dimensional Media (2013-2014, Semester 1)

This foundation-level studio course introduces the aesthetic, conceptual, and technical foundations of three-dimensional art-making and the ways three-dimensional media inform the creation and understanding of art in cultural contexts. Students explore three-dimensional form and space through a wide variety of materials and media. The course emphasizes strategies for idea generation. Students engage in dynamic activity, spirited investigation, and thoughtful creative expression. Materials fee. Offered each semester. Counts toward biomedical studies concentration (for students through class of 2016).
