ESTH 374: Biomechanics (2013-2014, Semester 1)

Students analyze mechanical principles in depth as they affect human motion. Topics include study of muscular and skeletal systems, skill analysis, and motion measurement techniques. The course includes a laboratory component. Prerequisite: Biology 143 or consent of instructor. Offered every year. Counts toward biomedical studies (available to students through class of 2016) and neuroscience concentrations.
