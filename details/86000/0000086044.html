MUSIC 264: Lyric Diction II (0.25) (2012-2013, Semester 2)

Students study and practice principles for singing in French and German with good pronunciation, enunciation, and expression. Course content includes phonetic analysis using the International Phonetic Alphabet and identifying and exercising the phonemic features of French and German through class performances and listening assignments. Prerequisite: Music 263 and two semesters of voice lessons. Offered spring semester 2012-13 and alternate years.
