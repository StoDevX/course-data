ART 229: Video Art (2011-2012, Summer Session 1)

This course focuses on the development of pieces of video art. Students study the rudiments of visual storytelling by developing their own short projects. Students study all aspects of production from concept to screening, including idea generation, pre-production planning, lighting, shooting, editing, and sound mixing, with special emphasis on innovative approaches to narrative form. Prerequisites: Art 104, Art 228, and basic Photoshop skills; or permission of instructor Materials fee. Offered alternate years. Counts toward film studies concentration.
