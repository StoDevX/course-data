GERM 371: Topics in Literature (2005-2006, Semester 1)

Explores the form, history and theory of a particular genre or medium, including film, the novella, young adult literature, drama, poetry and short story, or the works of a single author. Close reading, discussion, analysis and interpretation of texts. Taught in German. Prerequisite: minimum of one 270-level course (two recommended). May be repeated if topic is different. GE: FOL-G, ALS-L (ALS-A for film).
