ASIAN 211: Asian Conversations: Rice and Society in East Asia (2005-2006, Semester 1)

Using rice (and agriculture more generally) as a means of access, this course will introduce students to an examination of the societies, politics and economies of East Asian countries. Drawing on social science methods, it looks at traditional East Asian societies as well as the drive for modernization and the consequences that modernization has had on these societies. Prerequisite: Asian Studies 112 or permission of the instructor. Counts toward the Asian Studies major.
