CHEM 371: Physical Chemistry (2005-2006, Semester 1)

Students delve further into the topics of kinetics, thermodynamics and atomic and molecular structure that were introduced in the first-year courses. This course emphasizes the mathematical aspects of chemistry. Specific topics may include reaction mechanisms, homogeneous and heterogeneous catalysis, phase transitions, macromolecules, symmetry, spectroscopy and molecular orbital theory. Prerequisite: Chemistry 126. Previous completion of Physics 124 or 126 is recommended. Concurrent registration in Chemistry 357 is required. Offered both semesters.
