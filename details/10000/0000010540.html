FREN 251: Writing French (2005-2006, Semester 1)

This course provides students with intensive practice in various types of writing in French (e.g., summary, description, narration, letter-writing). Topics and model texts, including a selection of non-literary texts, are chosen by instructor. Taught in French, the course involves discussion, writing and revising and stresses advanced grammar review. Prerequisite: French 232, 233, or equivalent.
