MUSIC 345B: Advanced Study in Music History (2015-2016, Semester 2)

Students explore the historical development of musical concepts, styles, and repertoires in India, the Middle East, Indonesia, and China from the earliest recorded times to the present. While addressing the distinctive philosophical foundations of music-making in these areas and the impact of cultural encounters and changing systems of government, patronage, and mediation, students will gain a deeper understanding of the relationship between music, history, and culture. Prerequisites: MUSIC 241 and MUSIC 242.
