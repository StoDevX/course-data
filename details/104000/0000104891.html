EDUC 270: Exploring Teaching (off-campus) (2015-2016, Interim)

This course provides full-time placement in a school setting for students wanting to explore a career in teaching or gain greater understanding of teaching as a profession. Directed by host teachers, students may observe, assist within the classroom, tutor, teach, coach, attend faculty meetings and functions, and meet with school personnel. Students attend weekly seminars and complete assigned readings and reflective writings. Counts as the interim requirement for the non-licensure and TEFL certificate education concentration. Prerequisite: EDUC 260 or EDUC 290.
