MUSIC 267: Advanced Acting For The Lyric Stage (2006-2007, Semester 1)

Music 267 is a studio course focusing on advanced techniques of acting and singing for the musical stage with emphasis on opera. Students explore voice, movement, improvisation and characterization at an advanced level. They receive coaching in musical and dramatic style through solo and small ensemble literature and prepare scenes for class performance. May be repeated once. Prerequisites: Two semester of voice study and Theatre 131 (Acting for the Lyric Stage) or permission of instructor.
